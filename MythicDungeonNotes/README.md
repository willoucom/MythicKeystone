# Mythic Dungeon Notes

**Never tab out of the game again to check a strategy.**

Mythic Dungeon Notes is a lightweight World of Warcraft addon that puts boss strategies and dungeon tips directly in your game — accessible in one click, at any time.

---

## What it does

A small floating button sits on your screen. Click it to open a full guide panel with everything you need to clear a Mythic+ key: entrance tricks, boss mechanics, interrupt priorities, skip routes, and positioning tips.

- **Dungeon list view** — all dungeons laid out as vignette cards with their artwork
- **Automatic zone detection** — opening the panel while inside a dungeon goes straight to that dungeon's guide
- **Two-column layout** — section menu on the left, detailed notes on the right with maps and icons
- **Beginner / Advanced tips** — color-coded so veterans can skip the basics and newer players aren't overwhelmed
- **Draggable button and window** — put them wherever they fit your UI

## Usage

A small icon appears on your screen after loading in. You can drag it anywhere.

- **Left-click** the button to open or close the guide panel
- **Navigate** the dungeon list, then click a dungeon to open its guide
- **Select a section** from the left column to read the corresponding tips
- The window is movable — drag the title bar to reposition it
